• 0.8oz White Roux (50/50 works)
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 1 Cup Cheddar Cheese

My book says 40/60 fat/flour is good for roux, but this calls for 50/50, seems to work well.

Be careful combining the roux and the milk, temp shock causes clumps. do not overheat the roux.

Make into bechemel first, then add cheddar.
