Mise En Place
  • 4 (4oz) Porkchops, seasoned, dusted
  • 4 Slices bacon, thinly chopped
  • 1 Medium Onion (1/2 normal lg white), Diced
  • 1/2 Cup Chicken broth
  • 1/4 Cup Cream
Instructions
  • Using medium sautee pan, cook bacon, keep grease.
  • Caramelize onions in bacon fat.
  • Set caramelized onions and bacon aside, keep grease.
  • Sautee porkchops, 2.5-3 minutes each side.
  • Deglaze with broth, reduce, and add cream.
  • Bring to boil, then add onions + bacon. Combine thoroughly.
  • Spoon over porkchops and serve.
